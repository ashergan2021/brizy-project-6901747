Our Backstory

Minecraft@SST's group of passionate coders started this project during 2020's Circuit Breaker as they realised that the incoming Sec 1 students were not able to experience the school on-site for open house due to COVID-19. The team then decided to start this initiative for anyone to experience what SST is like, or even for current students or alumni who are school-sick.
